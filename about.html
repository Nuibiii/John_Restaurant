Our Filipino Heritage

John's Restaurant is more than just a dining experience - it's a celebration of Filipino culture and culinary tradition.

From Humble Beginnings

Our story began in 1995 in a small carinderia in Pampanga, known as the Culinary Capital of the Philippines. John Santos, our founder, started with just three dishes: adobo, sinigang, and kare-kare. His secret? Recipes passed down through generations of his family.

The Kapampangan Touch

Drawing from our Kapampangan roots, we've perfected the art of balancing flavors - the sweetness of coconut milk, the tang of tamarind, and the richness of peanut sauce. Each dish tells a story of our heritage.

Modern Filipino Cuisine

While we honor traditional recipes, we've also embraced innovation. Our chefs combine time-honored techniques with modern culinary trends, creating dishes that are both authentic and exciting.

Our Philosophy

We believe in "Lutong Bahay, Puso ng Pinoy" - home-cooked meals with the heart of the Filipino people. Every dish is prepared with the same love and care as in a Filipino home kitchen.
